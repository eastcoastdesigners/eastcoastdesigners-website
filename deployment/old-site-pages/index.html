FAQ

Answers to common renovation questions.

What types of projects are the best fit for East Coast Designers?

We focus on full-home renovations, major reconfigurations, and highly detailed kitchen and bath projects. Most of our work involves coordinating architecture, interiors, and construction for townhomes, apartments, and coastal residences across the East Coast.

When should we engage your team in the renovation process?

Ideally, we become involved as early as possible—before you finalize floor plans or engage a general contractor. This allows us to guide space planning, provide accurate finish-level budgets, and assemble the right team from the beginning.

Do you work with our contractor, or bring your own?

We are comfortable collaborating with a contractor you already trust or introducing vetted partners from our network. In either case, we provide drawings, specifications, and ongoing design oversight throughout construction.

What is a typical design and build timeline?

Timelines vary by scope and building approvals, but most full-home projects range from 9–18 months from initial consultation to final installation. We will provide a tailored timeline after we understand your specific project.
